CAREER SUMMARY

With over 20 years of experience as a registered architect including 5 years as senior designer for Skidmore, Owings & Merrill, a major architectural, engineering and planning firm, and 4 years as a Board Director for the American Lung Association, Richard left Portland in 1989 to manage global sales and marketing activity for a multi-national, Coors-owned, high-technology ceramic manufacturer with responsibility for generating sales and developing an international client base. Richard returned to architecture establishing and managing SOM's Hong Kong office in 1993 creating new project opportunities while directing the global firm's Asian strategic business development operations.

In 1996, Richard became Real Estate Development Director for AIG Real Estate's Asian projects before returning, once again, to SOM in 1998 as Managing Director of the Miami office with responsibility for developing new client contacts and projects throughout the Caribbean, Central and South America regions. Upon returning to Portland in 2000, Richard served as Principal with SERA Architects and Team Oregon in addition to serving on two non-profit Boards before starting Woodling Partners, Inc. and Green Touch Design in 2003, a green, healthy home design, community development and sustainable planning firm.

About Greentouch About GreenTouch
Our mission is to help individual property owners and human settlements prepare for and survive the transition to a world no longer endowed with a retrievable supply of fossil fuel.

Civilization is rapidly advancing towards a time when the extraction of fossil fuels will no longer be cost effective or practically achievable. Given today's global dependency on limited supplies of oil and natural gas, most homeowners and human settlements are ill equipped to deal with the drastic changes that will certainly take place once these finite, earth-bound resources become scarce and too costly to obtain in a rational manner. | Contact Contact
